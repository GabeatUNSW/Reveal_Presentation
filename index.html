Design Concept

The deisgn concept of Control and Freedom was inspired from the movie Akira. The movie is set in a post apocalyptic city where the government controls everything including the people and the people try to get out of the control through seeking freedom.

The Roof

The image shown on the right is from the very last scene of the movie where one of the main character turns into a monster. I depicted the monster's undefined and random shape into how the roof is designed.

Floor Plan

The building consists of first floor and the second floor and it's divided into three part. The middle part is the Theater and the two part on the side are the residence. The left side is the living room and kitchen space with big open space where the owner could invite friends to come over. The right side is the bedroom; 2 bedrooms on the second floor and one master bedroom on the first floor. Furthermore, inbetween the theater and the residence, there are gardens that has bamboo trees which fucntion as a place to relax and also as a spatial divider

The Bridge

The bridge is used to connect the two residence together into one and connects the living room space with the bedroom space. This also goes with the concept of freedom where the resident can freely move around and without interacting with the people in the theater.
